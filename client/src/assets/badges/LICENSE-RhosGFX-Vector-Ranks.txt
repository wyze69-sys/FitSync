----------------------------------------
Folder:  ./rhosgfx-ranks/   (see also ./rhosgfx-ranks/README.md)

source of truth used by the app for XP/level rank badges. The earlier
placeholder folder ./ranks/ has been removed to avoid duplication.

STREAK / FITNESS BADGE ART (./custom/)
--------------------------------------
The streak medals (streak-3, streak-7, streak-14, streak-30) and the category
medals (strength, cardio, consistency) are ORIGINAL FitSync-authored SVGs in a
deliberately different circular "achievement medal" style. They are NOT derived
from the RhosGFX pack, keeping the XP rank league system and the FitSync
achievement medals visually distinct on purpose.
